Sixteen different grids, each containing 3 Spanish words, will appear
one at a time. You will have 5 seconds once the grid appears to select and
study all of the items within the grid. Below each word will be a number, which tells you how
many points that word is worth. To see the English equivalent, simply
click on the Spanish word. Once you finish studying an item, click the “Close” button
in the bottom right of the screen which will bring you back to the grid
and give you the option to (re)select another item. If at any point a
word is not being displayed simply click the "Refresh" button in the
bottom left to reset the screen.

If you finish studying the items within a grid before time runs out, simply click the
“Continue” button to move on to the next set of items. Once you have
gone through the sixteen grids you will be asked to complete a final
recall test in which you will be presented with each Spanish word and
asked to type the English equivalent. Again, your goal is to EARN AS
MANY POINTS AS POSSIBLE.
