In this task you will be asked to try to remember numbers presented in different fonts.
You will be asked to complete two study-test trials. In each trial you will be shown 32
different numbers, one at a time, each for 5 seconds. Your goal is to memorize these numbers
for an upcoming recognition test. After you study each number you will be asked to rate how
likely you believe you will be to recognize the number on the recognition test.

During the recognition test you will be shown 64 numbers, half of which will be new and half
of which you will have studied. You will be asked to indicate whether you studied the number or not.

To advance to the next screen, please click on the word "continue" at the bottom of the
screen; however, be sure to read the instruction screens thoroughly before continuing
because you will be unable to go back.
