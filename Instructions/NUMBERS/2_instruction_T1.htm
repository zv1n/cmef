As noted, after you study each number, you will be asked to estimate how likely you will be
to remember the number on a later recognition test using a 0 to 100 scale where 0 = no confidence
and 100 = complete confidence that you will be able to recognize the number.

For example, you may be presented with a number such as "922" and then, after 5 seconds of
study time asked, "On a scale of 0 to 100, how likely are you to remember the number on the
upcoming recognition test?" You will be asked to type ANY NUMBER between 0 and 100 to indicate
your confidence level. Later, on the recognition task you will be shown 64 numbers and asked to
indicate for each number whether it is one you studied during the study phase or not.

There will be two trials each containing 32 numbers to study. At the end of each trial
you will be given a recognition test.

If you have any questions at this time, please raise your hand quietly to alert the research
assistant. If you are ready to begin then please click on the word "continue" at the bottom of the
screen.
