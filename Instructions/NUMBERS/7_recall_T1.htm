Now that you have completed the study phase for Trial 1, you are ready to begin the Trial 1
recognition test. Once you begin the test you will be shown 64 numbers, one at a time, and
asked to indicate whether the number is one you studied during the study phase or not.

If the number is one you studied, please press O for old. If it is a new number that you did
not see during the study phase, please press N for new. After you provide each old/new judgment
you will click on the continue button to bring up the next number.

Once you complete the Trial 1 recognition test, you will see instructions for Trial 2. If you have questions
please quietly notify the experimenter now. Otherwise, please click "Continue" to begin the recognition test.
