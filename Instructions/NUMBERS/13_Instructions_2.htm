Now that you have completed the study phase for Trial 2, you are ready to begin the Trial 2
recognition test. Once you begin the test you will be shown 64 numbers, one at a time, and
asked to indicate whether the number is one you studied during the Trial 2 study phase or not.

If the number is one you studied, please press O for old. If it is a new number that you did
not see during the study phase, please press N for new. After you provide each old/new judgment
you will click on the continue button to bring up the next number.

If you have questions please quietly notify the experimenter now. Otherwise, please click "Continue"
to begin the recognition test.
