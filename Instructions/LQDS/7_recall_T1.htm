Now that you have completed the study phase for Trial 1, you are ready to begin the Trial 1
recall test. Once you begin the test you should type each word you remember. You will type
one word, then press Continue to type the next word. Avoid typing more than one word in each
blank. Please spend about 5 minutes trying to recall as many words as possible. Once you feel
that you have recalled every word that you can remember, you will have to click on the continue
button multiple times (once for each of the 60 words you could not recall) until you see a screen
with text appear.

Once you complete the Trial 1 recall test, you will see instructions for Trial 2. If you have questions
please quietly notify the experimenter now. Otherwise, please click "Continue" to begin the recall test.
