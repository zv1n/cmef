As noted, after you study each word, you will be asked to estimate how likely you
think it is that you will be able to remember the word on a later recall test using
a 0 to 100 scale where 0 = no confidence and 100 = complete confidence that you will
be able to recall the word.

For example, you may be presented with a word such as "pumpkin" and then, after
5 seconds of study time asked, "On a scale of 0 to 100, how likely are you to remember the
word on the upcoming recall test?" You will be asked to type ANY NUMBER between 0 and 100
to indicate your confidence level. Later, on the recall task you will be asked to type all
the words you can recall from the study phase.

There will be two trials each containing 60 words. At the end of each trial
you will be given a recall test.

If you have any questions at this time, please raise your hand quietly to alert the research
assistant. If you are ready to begin then please click on the word "continue" at the bottom of the
screen.
