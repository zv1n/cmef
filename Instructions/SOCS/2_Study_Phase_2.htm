Sixteen different grids, each containing 3 Spanish words, will
appear one at a time. Above each word will be a number, which
tells you how many points that word it worth. To see the English
equivalent, simply click on the question mark beneath the Spanish
word. You may study the items as long as you would like. Once
you finish studying an item, click the “Done studying this item”
button, which will give you the option to (re)select another item.

Once you finish studying the items within a grid, simply click the
“Next Grid” button to move on to the next set of items. Once you
have gone through the sixteen grids you will be asked to complete
a final recall test in which you will be presented with each
Spanish word and asked to type the English equivalent. Again,
your goal is to EARN AS MANY POINTS AS POSSIBLE.
