Sixteen different grids, each containing 3 Spanish words, will
appear one at a time. You will have 5 seconds total per grid to
select and study all the items within the grid. The 5 seconds
will begin when the grid first appears. Above each Spanish word
will be a number, which tells you how many points that word it
worth. To see the English equivalent, simply click on the
question mark beneath the Spanish word. Once you finish studying
an item, click the “Done studying this item” button, which will
give you the option to (re)select another item if time still
remains.
If you finish studying the items within a grid before
time elapses, simply click the “Next Grid” button to move on to
the next set of items. Once you have gone through the sixteen
grids you will be asked to complete a final recall test in which
you will be presented with each Spanish word and asked to type
the English equivalent. Again, your goal is to EARN AS MANY
POINTS AS POSSIBLE. Please click "Continue".
