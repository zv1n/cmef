In this task you will be asked to try to remember words presented in different fonts.
Some words represent large items (e.g., building), while other words represent
smaller items that are small enough to fit in a shoebox (e.g., paperclip). The
words will either be presented in large 48 point font or small 18 point font. Thus,
in some cases you will study large items presented in large fonts, small items presented
in small fonts, or the size of the items and fonts will be mismatched (e.g., small items
will be presented in large font).

To advance to the next screen, please click on the word "continue" at the bottom of the
screen; however, be sure to read the instruction screens thoroughly before continuing
because you will be unable to go back.
