Now that you have completed the study phase for Trial 1, you are ready to begin the Trial 1
recall test. Once you begin the test you will be shown the first word of each pair (e.g., paperclip - ?)
and will type the second word that was matched with it during the study phase (e.g., yarn).

If you are unable to remember the missing word you should click "Continue" to move on to the next word.
Once you complete the Trial 1 recall test, you will see instructions for Trial 2. If you have questions
please quietly notify the experimenter now. Otherwise, please click "Continue" to begin the recall test.
