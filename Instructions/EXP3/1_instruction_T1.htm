In this task you will be asked to try to learn related (e.g., table - chair)
and unrelated (e.g., paperclip - yarn) word pairs presented in either large
48 point font or small 18 point font.

You will be presented with 60 different word pairs in each of two trials and will have
5 seconds to study each pair. You will be informed whether the word pair is
related/unrelated and in large/small font prior to studying each pair. You will also be
asked to predict the likelihood of you recalling each word pair.

To advance to the next screen, please click on the word "continue" at the bottom of the
screen; however, be sure to read the instruction screens thoroughly before continuing
because you will be unable to go back.
