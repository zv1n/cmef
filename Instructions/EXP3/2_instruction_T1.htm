After you study each word pair, you will be asked to estimate how likely you will be to remember
the word pairs on a later recall test using a 0 to 100 scale where 0 = no confidence and
100 = complete confidence that you will be able to recall the second word of the pair when
shown the first word of each pair.

For example, you may be presented with a word pair such as "paperclip - yarn" and then, after
5 seconds of study time asked, "On a scale of 0 to 100, how likely are you to remember the second
word of the pair when shown the first word on the upcoming recall test?" You will be asked to type
ANY NUMBER between 0 and 100 to indicate your confidence level. Later, on the recall task you will
be shown the first word, "paperclip - ?" and asked to type the second word of the pair, "yarn."

There will be two trials each containing 60 word pairs. At the end of each trial
you will be given a word pair recall test.
