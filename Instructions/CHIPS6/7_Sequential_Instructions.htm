In each study phase you will be able to decide which items to study.
Remember that you will have 30 seconds per grid to study items. If you click
on a Chinese character a new screen will open containing the Chinese character
and its English equivalent. A clock will begin counting down until you indicate
you are finished studying that item. You may select or reselect items to study
until time runs out. If you finish studying items before time runs out you can
move on to the next grid. Your goal is to prepare for the upcoming recall test in
which you will be presented with each Chinese character and asked to type what it
means in English. You will then receive instructions for Trial 2. We have provided
an example word pair below so that you know what to expect during the study phase.
