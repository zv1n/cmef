In each study phase you will be able to decide which items to study.
You will have 30 seconds total to study the words within a grid. Only by clicking on
a Chinese word will you be able to view the English equivalent for that word. Once
you select a word to study, a new screen will appear that contains the complete
Chinese-English vocabulary pair as well as a countdown clock indicating how much time
you have left to study words within the grid. This clock will continue to count down
until you either indicate you are finished studying the word by clicking the
"Close" button or time runs out.

Please click "Continue" to view a sample study screen.
