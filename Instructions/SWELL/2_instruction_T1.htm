As noted, YOUR GOAL IN THIS TASK IS TO EARN AS MANY POINTS AS
POSSIBLE. To do this you will select items to study and memorize.
You will be shown 5 different grids in each of two trials. Each
grid will contain 2 easy, 2 medium, and 2 difficult vocabulary pairs.
So you know each pair's difficulty level, they will be labeled either
easy, medium (moderately difficult), or difficult.

You will only see the Swahili portion of each vocabulary pair
(e.g., sala - ), and will have to click on that item in order to
see the English equivalent.

Please click on the word "Continue" at the bottom of the screen.
