As noted, YOUR GOAL IN THIS TASK IS TO EARN AS MANY POINTS AS POSSIBLE. To do this
you will select items to study and memorize. You will be shown 5 different grids
in each of two trials. Each grid will contain 2 easy, 2 medium, and 2 difficult
word pairs. So you know each pair's difficulty level, they will be labeled either
E for easy, M for medium (moderately difficult) items, or D for difficult. You
will only see the Swahili portion of each vocabulary pair (e.g., sala - ), and
will have to click on that item in order to see the English equivalent.

Please click on the word "continue" at the bottom of the screen.
