"On a scale of 0 to 100, how likely are you to remember the English equivalent on the
upcoming recall test?" You will be asked to type ANY NUMBER between 0 and 100 to indicate
your confidence level. Later, on the recall task you will be shown each Swahili word and
asked to type the English equivalent.

the allotted time. At the end of each trial you will be given a recall test.
